How can I create an account on Davoxel?

To create an account on Davoxel, simply click the "Sign up" icon on the top right corner of the homepage, fill in your basic details, and click "Submit".

What types of files can I upload to Davoxel?

You can upload 3D models, textures, animations, and other digital assets that are compatible with the formats we support, including .obj, .fbx, .blend, .ma, .max, .ztl, and .stl. Please ensure that your files are optimized, clean, and error-free.

How do I price my products?

You have full control over the pricing of your products on Davoxel. We recommend that you consider the complexity, quality, and market demand of your products when setting the price. You can also check the prices of similar products on our platform for reference.

How does Davoxel handle copyright and intellectual property rights?

Davoxel respects the intellectual property rights of artists and clients. By uploading your products to Davoxel, you retain the ownership and copyright of your work, and you grant Davoxel a non-exclusive license to sell and distribute your products on our platform. We also have measures in place to prevent copyright infringement and piracy.

How do I get paid for my sales on Davoxel?

Davoxel pays artists via PayPal. You'll need to link your PayPal account to your Davoxel account to receive payments.

How much does it cost to sell on Davoxel?

It is free to sign up as a creator on Davoxel. Davoxel charges a commission of 30% on each sale you make. This means that if you sell a product for $100, you'll receive $65 and Davoxel will receive $30 and $5 goes to payment gateways like Stipe or PayPal.

How do I know when I have made a sale on Davoxel?

You will receive an email notification when you make a sale on Davoxel. You can also check your sales history on your dashboard.

Can I sell products that I've already sold on other platforms?

Yes, you can sell products that you've already sold on other platforms. However, you'll need to make sure that you're not violating any exclusive distribution agreements you may have with other platforms.

Can I offer discounts or promotions for my products on Davoxel?

Yes, you can offer discounts or promotions for your products on Davoxel using Coupons. This can be a great way to attract new customers and increase your sales.

What happens if my product is downloaded illegally?

Davoxel takes copyright infringement very seriously and has policies in place to protect the intellectual property rights of its artists. If your product is downloaded illegally, you should contact Davoxel's support team immediately to report the issue.
